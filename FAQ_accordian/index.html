Frequently Asked Questions

What is the return policy?
You can return items within 30 days of receiving them for a full refund or exchange. Items must be in their original condition with tags and packaging.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept a variety of payment methods to make your shopping experience convenient. These include:
  • Credit and Debit Card
  • UPI Payments
How can I track my order?
Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. To track your order:
  • Go to the "Order Tracking" section on our website.
  • Enter your tracking number in the provided field.
  • Click on the "Track" button to see the current status of your shipment.
